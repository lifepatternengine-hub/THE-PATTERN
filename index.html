The Model — Three phases 01 / 03
01
Phase 01

Friction

Something stops fitting. The role is the same. The output continues. But internally, something is wrong that no-one else can see — and you cannot name it. Performance persists while alignment collapses.

Signal: everything works
and nothing fits
02
Phase 02

Dissolution

Identity structures collapse. Role, status, certainty — gone. The freeze. The hollow. The silence in meetings where you used to speak with authority. The structures that held the self are no longer load-bearing.

Signal: the structures that held
the self are no longer load-bearing
03
Phase 03

Reconstruction

Deliberate rebuilding from a different foundation. Not recovery to the previous state. Not healing. Reconfiguration — into something that holds more of who you actually are.

Signal: building from values,
not from role or institution
